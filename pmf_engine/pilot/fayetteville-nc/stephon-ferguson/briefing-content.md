You endorsed Chief Roberto Bryan's community-engagement approach and proposed consolidating youth, safety, and anti-crime initiatives into a single organizational hub. Monday's update is the right moment to ask where that consolidation stands and whether the quarterly metrics reflect progress toward root-cause interventions.
